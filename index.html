Our Story

Since 2014

Butcher's Burger started as an Egyptian success story, evolving from a small eatery into a prestigious brand specializing in fresh meat and burgers. It stands out for serving burgers made from premium cuts using a unique "smashing" technique to achieve a rich flavor and perfect crust. The brand has successfully expanded its menu to include various options like steak and brisket

  • Flame-grilled burgers, made fresh
  • There are currently around 22 branches in Egypt
  • A place for families & friends
